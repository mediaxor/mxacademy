Modeling the future by providing
cutting-edge technology training

Cutting edge technology is a mere application of fundamentals of different disciplines, so as to find solution to a specific problem definition through research. Our education system is designed to focus on fundamentals of these disciplines however, quality of education is achieved when learning of fundamentals is supplemented with the learnings through research. Consequently, acquiring skills and knowledge beyond the learning of fundamentals is necessary to be competitive before entering the industry. Hands-on learning is extremely important, especially with the same tools, platforms and technologies that are used in industry.

Training is an opportunity for reinvigorating and extending fundamentals with new paradigms.

Multimedia technologies have experienced a rapid growth within the last few years and have attained a high economic importance worldwide. There are numerous examples in digital speech, audio and video coding and processing, digital transmission and communication networks. From concept through research and development, creating embedded multimedia products is tough work.

Embedded product development is a multiple stage process involving concept, research and development. Embedded systems pose unique technical challenges, particularly related to power, performance and memory footprint. Our trainings offer to build an understanding around these processes and solutions to overcome these challenges.
