  • Embedded Multimedia

    Embedded industry is undergoing a paradigm shift from fixed function to convergence of functions on a single device. Advanced processors are designed to optimize power and performance across CPU, GPU and DSP in an integrated package such that technologies like multimedia can be put into use for so many products and applications.

Mediaxor Academy specializes in imparting skills and knowledge required to advance the state of art in embedded multimedia domain. We offer technology based skill development and domain oriented trainings, to generate a competent pool of talent and prepare them for industry. Mediaxor academy aims to build a community of multimedia engineers by providing trainings to students and collaborating with academia and industry for promoting multimedia related research and development activities in the community.

Consumer electronic products continue to evolve and transform into truly smart, secure and connected systems.

Automotive(s) are undergoing a massive change. Cars are being transformed into a connected multimedia experience.

At the heart of these multimedia devices are advanced System On Chips that are designed to bring power efficiency and ultra sophisticated multimedia experience to the embedded system.

Consumer electronics is about taking into account the features of these advanced System on Chips and transforming them into products.

Products are designed by researching and developing new techniques that guarantee both the functionality and quality of the product. Such techniques demand strong understanding of the fundamentals of technology and skills for implementing their solutions.

Multimedia is everywhere. Generally, the term multimedia is used for entertainment purpose only. For example, watching videos over Youtube, streaming movies over Netflix, streaming songs over Spotify / Wync / Soundcloud or taking pictures in Instagram. However, multimedia finds its application in different areas including advertisement, education, publishing, business, medicine, art, engineering and research. In context of communication technology such as VOIP and video conferencing, multimedia is a field of specialization in itself. Unfortunately, there is not much acceptance of multimedia as a discipline of engineering due to its entertainment aspect which hampers accepting it as a discipline of engineering in academics and industry. However, as products evolve the demand for rightly skilled and ready to deploy workforce continues to increase and industry continues to face challenges in finding the right talent.
