Who we are

Mediaxor Academy is a specialist in real time embedded multimedia software development, providing training to clients in a wide range of industry sectors.

With every industry now manufacturing products that contain embedded computer systems, software has become the critical factor dominating embedded systems design. Multimedia technology is at the heart of every product that has a display or audio. In order to remain competitive in this constantly changing market, it is critical to continuously strengthen the competency and expertise in designing and developing these embedded systems.

Mediaxor Academy is the training division of Mediaxor Technologies Private Limited and specializes in imparting skills and knowledge required to advance the state of art in embedded multimedia domain.

What we do

Many embedded product manufacturers are not traditionally software developers. Engineers graduating from university are ill equipped for embedded development. Professionals working in industry need to upate with latest trends in technology. Mediaxor Academy helps plug this crucial skills gap.

We enhance and improve the competence of embedded software professionals and students by:

- Training and Mentoring

- Educating fundamentals of technology

- Upskilling to use relevant tools

- Reverse engineering established solutions

- Researching and implementing alternate designs

- Collaborating on problem definitions

Our mission is to build an ecosystem around embedded

multimedia technologies and promote multimedia related

research and development in the community.

We are different

We are just not a training institute. We are an academy and we work in complex areas of electronics and computer engineering. Our approach is professional, we focus on research and we believe in collaboration.

Our trainings are pragmatic and are delivered by industry experts, some of whom are actively working in their area of specialization.

We enhance skills and improve understanding of fundamentals that prepare you for industry. Our trainings prepare you to aim for premium job opportunities in embedded industry.
